# Product Backlog
- As a customer, I want to see all products (like bed desks and back cushions) so that I can find items for my back pain.
- As a customer, I want to view product details, prices, and stock so that I can decide what to buy.
- As a customer, I want to add items to my cart and choose the quantity so that I am ready to pay.
- As a customer, I want to place an order by entering my name, email, and phone number so that the shop can deliver my items.
- As a customer, I want to pay for my order so that my purchase is complete.
- As a customer, I want to open a support ticket so that I can ask for help from the admin.
- As an admin, I want to add, edit, or delete products so that the shop always shows correct information.
- As an admin, I want to view all orders and update their status so that I can pack and ship them correctly.
- As an admin, I want to read and reply to support tickets so that I can help customers with their problems.
